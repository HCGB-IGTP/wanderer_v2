Wanderer
An interactive viewer to explore DNA methylation
and gene expression data in human cancer


Examples: BRCA1 or ENSG00000012048 Tip: Gene symbols look like TP53 or BRCA1 whereas Ensembl Gene IDs start by ENSG

Dataset

We offer level 3 TCGA data for methylation arrays (450k Infinium chip) and expression (Illumina HiSeq RNAseq, summarized by exons).
